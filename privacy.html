Privacy Policy for FleetLinkr

Last updated:

NYC TLC Compliance Notice: FleetLinkr adheres to New York City Taxi and Limousine Commission regulations regarding data collection for vehicle listings and driver verification.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following TLC-required data:

  • Driver Information: TLC license number, expiration date
  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, TLC plate number, inspection status
  • Rental Records: Dates, durations, and parties involved in transactions

2. Data Use & Sharing

Data may be shared with:

  • NYC TLC for compliance audits
  • Insurance providers for verification
  • Law enforcement if required by subpoena

  • File complaints about data misuse
